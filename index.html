The Energetic Framework

AutisticScholars advances a multi-level analytic framework examining cognition as metabolically constrained and socially structured. The model integrates neuroenergetics, precision regulation theory, and structural analysis to investigate burnout as cumulative energetic imbalance.

Biological Scale

Investigating neuroenergetic constraints on excitatory–inhibitory balance and metabolic limits on sustained high-fidelity processing.

Interactional Scale

Analyzing masking as metabolic labor and the asymmetric inferential effort required in coupled social systems.

Structural Scale

Examining institutional expectations and chronic extraction as drivers of systemic energetic insolvency.

About AutisticScholars

AutisticScholars is a research-led institute advancing cross-scale analysis of autistic cognition. Our work integrates computational modeling and neuroenergetics within a framework designed to generate testable hypotheses for interdisciplinary scholarship.

Research Lead

Current research initiatives focus on the intersection of predictive processing theory, metabolic labor, and the structural conditions that shape autistic burnout.
